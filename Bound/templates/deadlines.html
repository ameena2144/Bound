Deadline Management Best Practices
Court Deadlines:
  • Never Miss Court: Failure to appear can result in default judgment
  • Arrive Early: Plan to arrive 15-30 minutes before scheduled time
  • Bring Documents: Have all required paperwork organized and ready
  • Dress Appropriately: Professional attire shows respect for the court
Filing Deadlines:
  • File Early: Don't wait until the last minute to submit documents
  • Confirm Receipt: Get proof of filing from the court clerk
  • Serve Copies: Ensure other parties receive required documents
  • Follow Rules: Court rules are strict about format and timing
